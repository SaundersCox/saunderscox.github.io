About

Hey! I'm Saunders, a transfer student with senior standing studying Computer Science at Virginia Tech. I live north of campus-It's a perfect location with nice views on walks, just a minute from the highway, a few minutes from Blacksburg Skatepark, and a five minute bus commute to campus.

I first came to Tech as a transfer student with absolutely no connections. I quickly made other newcomer friends and brought them together.

I've always loved integrating my life with technology. Whether it be video games, music production, long distance connections, planning trips, or maintaining a diet, I've always had computers to assist me. From my elementary school years, I was first introduced to HTML and CSS through my generation's social media: forums, and personalized webpages. In my Neopets shop, I'd embed a MIDI file of some catchy TV intro into my virtual store. On my GaiaOnline profile, I'd modify the CSS to get my first taste of web design.

Fast forward to today, I've created various personal Java projects. Most of these scenarios prompted from college courses have given me some insight on the applications I have the potential to build. As a result, I can envision how I can contribute in a team environment. I love expressing my creative side when applicable, and I prefer to approach solutions with an open mind.

Additionally, I've become familiar with some of the quirkiness of C and the streamlined nature of Python. My Fall 2019 courses consist of CS 3114: Data Structures and Algorithms--mostly partnered projects in Java, CS 2506: Intro to Computer Organization (Part 2)--C labs and circuitry studies, CS 3654: Data Analytics and Visualization--Pandas in Python and manipulating datasets.
